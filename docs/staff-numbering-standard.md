# ASHE TOKUN Employee Number Standard

Employee numbers are permanent operational identifiers for ASHE TOKUN staff.

## Ranges

- `0001-0099`: Executive Leadership
- `0100-0199`: Managers / Supervisors
- `0200-0299`: Cashiers / Sales
- `0300-0399`: Inventory / Receiving
- `0400-0499`: Shipping / Fulfillment
- `0500-0599`: Customer Service
- `0600-0699`: Administration / Accounting / Finance
- `0700-0799`: Marketing / E-Commerce
- `0800-0899`: Operations / Maintenance
- `0900-0999`: Temporary / Seasonal / Contractors

## Rules

- Employee numbers are exactly four digits.
- Employee numbers are stored as text.
- Employee numbers are never changed.
- Employee numbers are never reused.
- Role changes do not change employee numbers.
- Staff with business history are archived, not deleted.

## Executive Leadership

- `0001`: Eduardo Gomez, Owner.
- `0002`: Felix Aleman, Managing Partner.

The Owner and Managing Partner are business titles. Security roles are separate
permission templates. The Owner uses the `owner` role. The Managing Partner uses
the `managing_partner` role.

AJAKO ORIGINALS and EDIBERE CREATION are vendors, suppliers, and manufacturers in ASHE TOKUN commerce context only. They are not ASHE TOKUN staff departments.
